Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long does it take for carpets to dry after a deep cleaning?

Typically, carpets are dry within 4 to 8 hours. We use high-powered extraction and air movers to ensure you can return to your normal routine as quickly as possible.

2. What is the benefit of a garage epoxy floor?

An epoxy garage floor is non-porous and resistant to oil, chemicals, and impact. It creates a seamless barrier that is much easier to clean than raw concrete.

3. Can you install new flooring over my existing tiles?

In many cases, yes. Our technicians can assess if a floating floor like laminate flooring installation can be placed over your current surface, saving you the time and cost of a full demolition.

4. How often should I have my hardwood floors refinished?

Most hardwoods benefit from a floor refinishing every 7 to 10 years, depending on the volume of foot traffic and the presence of pets.

5. Do you offer commercial-scale services?

Yes, we are full-service commercial flooring contractors equipped to handle warehouses, retail spaces, and corporate offices nationwide.
