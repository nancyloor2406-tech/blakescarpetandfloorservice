Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the "1/4 rule" regarding hardwood installation in Wagner, SD?

This rule refers to the expansion gap we leave around the perimeter of the room. Because wood is a natural material that expands and contracts with the humidity in Wagner, SD, this 1/4 inch space prevents the floor from buckling or crowning over time.

Yes, for our commercial clients in Wagner, SD, we provide full documentation regarding the removal and responsible disposal of old materials. This is often required for environmental compliance and internal property records.
